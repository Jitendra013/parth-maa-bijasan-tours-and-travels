LOGO PLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Please save your company logo in this folder with the name:
- logo.png (preferred)
OR
- logo.jpg

The logo will be automatically displayed in:
1. Navigation bar (top left)
2. Hero section (center, above the title)
3. Footer (top of footer section)

Recommended logo specifications:
- Format: PNG (with transparent background) or JPG
- Size: 400x200 pixels or similar aspect ratio
- File size: Under 200KB for fast loading
- Background: Transparent PNG works best

If the logo file is not found, the website will automatically show text-based branding instead.
