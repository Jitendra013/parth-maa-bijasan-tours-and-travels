About Us

Your Trusted Travel Partner

Welcome to Parth Maa Bijasan Tours & Travels, your premier destination for reliable and comfortable taxi services across India. We are dedicated to providing exceptional travel experiences with a focus on safety, comfort, and customer satisfaction.

Founded and operated by Karuna Shukla and Jitendra Shukla, our travel agency has been serving customers with local and All India taxi services. We understand that every journey matters, and we strive to make each trip memorable and hassle-free.

Our Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and affordable travel solutions that exceed customer expectations. We believe in building lasting relationships through exceptional service and reliability.
